PC components

Component Original chip Significance
CPU Intel 8088 All future chips are (nearly) completely compatible with this chip.
Interrupt controller Intel 8259A Converts simple electrical IRQs from devices into interrupt request bus cycles that the CPU understands.
Timer Intel 8253 Provides the DOS clock (prior to the introduction of the real-time clock chip), refreshed DRAM & drives the PC speaker.
Keyboard controller Intel 8042 Converts keyboard signals into CPU i/o data. Also used for a selection of non-keyboard things, e.g. A20 line control.
Graphics MGA, CGA, EGA, VGA
